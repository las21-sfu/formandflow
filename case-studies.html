AFFORDANCES

Affordances are the physical properties of an object that suggest how it should be used, essentially speaking to your intuition without words. This hinged switch features a protruding mechanism that signals its function through its shape. By clicking it upward, you engage the intended action, transforming the dark environment into an illuminated space. Good design ensures that the correct parts are visible and convey a message so that labels become unnecessary . When you see a mechanism like this, you don’t have to think—the object simply explains its own operation.
