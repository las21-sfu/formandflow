Tying it Together

DESIGN IS
NEVER
NEUTRAL.

01
Norman Said

Every time you struggle with a product, it is the designer's failure — not yours. Bad design teaches us to feel helpless.

02
Fraser Said

We are drowning in objects. The ethical designer asks not just "does it work?" but "should it exist? Can it be repaired? Will it last?"

03
Our Manifesto

Design against confusion. Every object is a conversation between maker and user. Make it a clear one.

The One-Way Door

YOU CANNOT
UN-SEE IT NOW.

You’ll start to notice these moments: a door that gives the wrong signal, controls that aren’t clearly labeled, interfaces that are harder than they need to be. When something feels frustrating, it’s often a design issue—not a user failure.
